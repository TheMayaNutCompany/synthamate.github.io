What we sell

Ice cream with candy inside—mixed throughout, not just sprinkled on top. From chocolate chunks to rainbow candy pieces, we build “swirls” that stay crunchy and sweet from first bite to last.

Where we’re based

Located in Palm Beach, Florida, we’re focused on bringing an ice-cream-truck vibe to the neighborhood with bright flavors and big candy energy.

About Candy Swirl

Candy Swirl is a Palm Beach ice cream brand built around one idea: candy belongs inside the ice cream, not just on top. We combine smooth bases with mix-ins that add crunch, color, and nostalgia.

Our vibe

Bright, playful, and classic—like pulling up to an ice cream truck on a sunny day. That’s why we went with a bold multicolor background and clean white panels for that “frosty” feel.
